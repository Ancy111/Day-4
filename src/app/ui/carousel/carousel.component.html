Mysore Palace

A former royal residence and one of the largest palaces in India, built in 1912. It's also known as Amba Vilas.

Taj Mahal

A white marble mausoleum built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most popular attractions in India.

Gateway of India

A famous monument on the Arabian Sea that was built in 1911.
